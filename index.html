Anne Hutter

Theoretical Astrophysicist at the University of Vienna exploring early galaxy evolution and reionisation.

My research focuses on understanding the formation and evolution of the first galaxies in the Universe, their imprint in the heating and ionisation of the intergalactic medium during Cosmic Dawn and the Epoch of Reionisation, and how combining galaxy and 21cm signal observations can reveal the nature of these first galaxies.
