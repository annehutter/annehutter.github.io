Anne Hutter

Postdoctoral researcher at the Kapteyn Astronomical Institute in Groningen studying the Epoch of Reionization.

I am a theoretical and computational astrophysicist, focusing on understanding the nature of the first galaxies in our Universe, their imprint in the ionization topology during the Epoch of Reionization and the impact of reionization on subsequent galaxy formation and evolution.
